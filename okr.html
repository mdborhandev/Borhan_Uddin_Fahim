About this Project

OKR (Objectives and Key Results) is a goal management platform that helps teams set, track, and achieve their objectives. It promotes alignment, focus, and accountability across organizations.

Key Features

OKR framework for setting company, team, and individual objectives

Key Results tracking with progress metrics

Task management linked to objectives

Weekly check-ins and progress updates

Performance dashboards with visual analytics

Team alignment views and dependency mapping

Integration with project management tools

Reporting and insights for leadership
