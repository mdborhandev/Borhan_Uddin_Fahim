About this Project

A full-scale Enterprise Resource Planning system designed to streamline and integrate core business processes across multiple departments. This ERP solution provides a unified platform for managing inventory, supply chain, human resources, finance, and operational workflows in real-time.

Key Features

Inventory management with real-time stock tracking and automated reordering

Supply chain management with vendor management and purchase order tracking

Financial management including general ledger, accounts payable/receivable

Human resources module for employee records, payroll, and performance

Manufacturing resource planning and production scheduling

Advanced reporting and analytics dashboard with customizable reports

Role-based access control with multi-level approval workflows

Integration capabilities with third-party systems and APIs
