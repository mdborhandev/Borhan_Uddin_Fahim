Halda - SaaS HRM Platform

Employee management, payroll, attendance & performance modules with RBAC

SaaS Cloud-Based RBAC

About this Project

Halda is a cloud-based Human Resource Management System designed to automate and streamline HR operations for organizations of all sizes. It covers the complete employee lifecycle from recruitment to retirement.

Key Features

Employee database with comprehensive profiles and documents

Attendance tracking with biometric integration

Leave management with approval workflows

Payroll processing with tax calculations and deductions

Performance management with goal setting and evaluations

Recruitment and applicant tracking system

Training and development module

Role-based access control with granular permissions

Technologies Used

ASP.NET Core MVC

PostgreSQL
