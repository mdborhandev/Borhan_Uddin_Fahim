Atrai - Accounting Software

Full-featured accounting software for financial management

About this Project

Atrai is a comprehensive accounting solution designed for small to medium businesses to manage their financial operations efficiently. It provides complete bookkeeping capabilities, financial reporting, and compliance tools in an intuitive interface.

Key Features

General ledger with double-entry bookkeeping system

Invoicing and billing with customizable templates

Expense tracking and receipt management

Bank reconciliation and transaction management

Financial statements: Balance Sheet, P&L, Cash Flow

Tax calculation and VAT/GST compliance

Multi-currency support for international transactions

Automated financial reports and analytics
